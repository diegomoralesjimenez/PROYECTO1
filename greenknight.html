The Green Knight

PG-13 | 130 min | Fantasy, Adventure | 30 July 2021 (USA)


Synopsis

The story goes that Sir Gawain (Dev Patel), one of the knights of King Arthur's Round Table, accepts the challenge of a Green Knight, who challenges any knight to brandish his sword against him. In return, the knight who accepts the challenge will receive the same blow back one year and one day later. Sir Gawain accepts the challenge and beheads the Green Knight, only for said knight to take his head and leave. But the Green Knight will remind Sir Gawain of the end of his deal when the deadline is up.
